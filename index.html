what is webtee?


we are an apparel company that specializes in working with NFTs and DAOs, composed of a team of designers, developers, and marketing experts.

we are committed to creating merchandise campaigns and storefronts that are of the highest quality and value.


we have a long history working in the web2 industry, running successful stores for popular youtube channels, like Techsmartt and Nightcore Zeus, along with other major influencers like PokemonGoNews, ReviewTechUSA, and Team Synergy.



what do we offer?


we offer fully customizable storefronts, merchandise production, and distribution.

there is a wide variety of products, ranging from mens, womens, and kids clothing, to acessories like coffee mugs and backpacks.


we also offer merch and promotional image designs, however we prefer if the NFT artist or DAO community designs and votes on the artwork.
